MANA HOTEL

The Mana Hotel, is a Heritage, Five star, luxury hotel built in the Saracenic Revival style in the Shuddhowala region of Dehradun, Uttarakhand, India.
THis Hotel with its 20 rooms and 2 suites is considered the flagship property property of the group; It employes some 30 staff. The Mana Hotel commits utself to the overall improvement of the ecological environment, which we are all a part of. We recongnize that we are not the owners but caretakers of the planet and owe it to our children and future generations of Humankind. It is our endeavour not only to conserve and protect but also to renew and regenerate the environment in which we live and operate. Our commitment encompasses all actions related to our products, services, associates, parterns, vendors and communities.
